Every morning I read a newspaper which gives me the news in the same boring fashion of a bundle of 15-20 pages contain various sections and graphic images to support the content. I would like to design an online system where all I have to do every morning is enter a URL into my web browser. The website automatically starts speaking the headlines for the day to me. The content is aggregated from various sources on the web and instead of one graphic image, each article could have a graphic image which keeps changing, thus using the images from all resources. This way I could do other tasks while I look at my screen from time to time and hear the news out. When i find an interesting article I could click on it get more information about it and read more.

I am designing a web service that gives you news on a website, but a little different that the standard news websites. This website has voice as well and will read out the headlines to you once the page is loaded (one of my latent needs). Please see my previous week posts if you want to know more about the project, the user needs and the prototypes that I had developed. My design project is a web-service, it is very difficult to code the complete web service in the timeframe of this course. But I have made an effort to the best of my possibilities. This is a beta prototype so it may not be 100% complete. I have a beta prototype ready : http://news.aashrayarora.com/ . Click on the link to see my website live! Go ahead !
